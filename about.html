About us

We are two Norwegian students at The University of Manchester. Back in Norway, hiking and being out in nature is a big part of our life. However, we found it difficult to locate what opportunities for hiking there is around the Manchester area. Therefore, we made this webside in hope that it would make it easier for current and future students to embrace the outdoors
